GCSE Mathematics – Algebra (WJEC Intermediate Tier)

This unit contains practice questions covering all Algebra content required for the WJEC GCSE Intermediate Tier.

A1: Algebraic Notation & Simplification

1. Simplify: 3x + 5x

Answer

8x

2. Simplify: 7a − 2a + 4

Answer

5a + 4

3. Simplify: 4y × 3

Answer

12y

4. Simplify: 6p ÷ 3

Answer

2p

A2: Expanding Brackets

1. Expand: 3(x + 4)

Answer

3x + 12

2. Expand: 5(a − 2)

Answer

5a − 10

3. Expand: −2(y + 6)

Answer

−2y − 12

4. Expand: 4(2x − 3)

Answer

8x − 12

A3: Factorising

1. Factorise: 6x + 12

Answer

6(x + 2)

2. Factorise: 5a − 15

Answer

5(a − 3)

3. Factorise: 4y + 8y²

Answer

4y(1 + 2y)

4. Factorise: −3p − 6

Answer

−3(p + 2)

A4: Substitution

Answer

10

7

Answer

18

4. Find the value of (x + 1)² when x = 5

Answer

36

A9: Rearranging Formulae

1. Make x the subject: y = 3x + 4

Answer

x = (y − 4) ÷ 3

2. Make a the subject: P = 2a + b

Answer

a = (P − b) ÷ 2

3. Make r the subject: A = πr²

Answer

r = √(A ÷ π)

4. Make h the subject: V = lwh

Answer

h = V ÷ (lw)
